SDG 3: Good Health & Well-Being

Target & Core Objectives

Sustainable Development Goal 3 aims to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages. Key objectives include:

  • Reducing global maternal and neonatal mortality rates.
  • Ending epidemics of AIDS, tuberculosis, malaria, and tropical diseases.
  • Strengthening prevention and treatment of substance abuse.
  • Supporting mental health, universal healthcare coverage, and medical research.
"Health is a state of complete physical, mental and social well-being and not merely the absence of disease." — WHO
